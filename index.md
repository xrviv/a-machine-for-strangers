## A fictionalized, source-annotated account of how the original Bitcoin software may have been written—and the world into which it arrived.

This is historical fiction grounded in the original Bitcoin v0.1 source code.

The imagined scenes and inner monologues are explicitly fictional. Each chapter will
separate them from the evidence: code locations, contemporary documents, and notes about
what can—and cannot—be known about Nakamoto.
